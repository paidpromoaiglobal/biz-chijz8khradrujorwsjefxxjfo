About Us

Style On Men salon

Style On Men Salon is Kelambakkam's trusted destination for modern men's grooming, located conveniently above Medplus Medical on Bajanai Koil Street, Veeranam Salai, Sathankuppam. With a team of skilled stylists dedicated to quality and precision, we ensure every client walks out looking and feeling their best — worth every rupee spent.

Open seven days a week from 8:30 AM to 9:30 PM, we make expert grooming accessible at your convenience. Whether it's a fresh haircut, a clean shave, or a complete makeover, our warm and professional team — led by talented stylists like Suhan — is committed to delivering an experience that keeps you coming back. Join our growing family of satisfied clients and discover why Style On Men is the go-to salon for men in Chennai's Kelambakkam area.
